Featured Projects

Select work demonstrating CAD design, aerodynamic analysis, and structural validation.

Privateer — Full Aircraft CAD Model

Complete SolidWorks assembly of a rubber-band-powered balsa aircraft designed for indoor competition. Detailed part modeling of wing ribs, spars, fuselage bulkheads, empennage, and propulsion linkage with lightening holes throughout.

SolidWorks Assembly Design GD&T

Privateer — CFD Pressure Distribution

ANSYS Fluent static pressure contour mapping over the full aircraft geometry. Visualizes pressure differential across wing surfaces, identifying lift-generating regions and stagnation points at competition flight conditions.

ANSYS Fluent CFD Pressure Mapping

Privateer — CFD Velocity Pathlines

Velocity magnitude pathline visualization showing flow behavior around the aircraft. Color-mapped streamlines reveal flow acceleration over the wing, wake structure behind the fuselage, and empennage interaction effects.

ANSYS Fluent Flow Visualization Post-Processing

Privateer — Wing Spar FEA

ANSYS Mechanical static structural analysis of the wing spar assembly. Von Mises equivalent stress distribution under simulated flight loads, with peak stress of 821 psi identified at the wing root constraint region.

ANSYS Mechanical FEA Stress Analysis

Phloat — Product Design CAD

SolidWorks CAD model of Phloat, a MagSafe-attached phone flotation device featuring a CO₂-actuated inflatable bladder. Designed for manufacturing with smooth lofted surfaces and integrated branding elements.
